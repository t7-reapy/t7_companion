## Why CC-BY-SA-4.0 and not MIT

The wiki pages are *synthesized derivatives* of community knowledge from community wikis, modding forums, and Discord — material whose individual contributors retain copyright. (YouTube transcripts also flow into wiki pages as cited references, while shipping as-is in `t7kb.db`.) Share-alike ensures downstream derivatives stay equally open — the community's collective contribution doesn't get fenced off by anyone forking the wiki into a closed-source product.
